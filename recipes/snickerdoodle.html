Snickerdoodle Cookies Recipe

Description

What sets a snickerdoodle cookie apart from the rest? Cinnamon sugar. These scrumptious treats are rolled in cinnamon sugar before being popped in the oven.

Ingredients

Cookies:

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Coating:

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease.

  2. Make cookies: Beat together white sugar, butter, and eggs in a large bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; stir into creamed butter mixture until dough holds together.

  3. Form dough into balls (about 2 teaspoons in each ball), then roll in cinnamon-sugar coating. Place dough balls about 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven on the center rack for 7 minutes. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
